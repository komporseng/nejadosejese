Hailey Malles On Bachelor 26

As we said, Hailey Malles is among the contestants who are trying to win the hearts of Clayton Echards in the 26th season of the Bachelor. She announced herself as a cast member of the Bachelor on 16 December on her Instagram with captions “The rumors are true…” followed by a rose emoji.

She also requested her followers to tune in to the show to “watch [her] take [her] chance on love”. The 26th season is set to premiere on 3 January 2022 on ABC.

Her bio on ABC’s website says “Hailey has a magnetic personality, is serious about finding love and won’t settle for someone who just looks perfect on paper.” Apparently, she is in search of a “family-oriented man”. She also wants her perfect man to have some career ambition and a “close relationship with God”.

Hailey wishes that her “dream man” would respect her for her personality and value her. She is a “firecracker” of a woman and he should not try to “soften” her. And she is trying to find her perfect man “by any means possible”.

However, it appears as if her The Bachelor journey was not totally successful. On 7 December, Reality Steve predicted the top three of Clayton’s Bachelor season. Apparently, the three women that Clayton found best for him are Gabby Windey, Rachel Recchia, and Susie Evans.

The upcoming season’s leading man, Clayton Echard, is a former football player from Missouri. He played football for the University of Missouri and was part of the Seattle Seahawks’ line but did not get a chance to play in NFL. Eventually, he quit football because he realized “his football abilities didn’t quite match his business acumen”.

Clayton currently works in marketing and business. You can read more about him here.

Hailey Malles Job

Hailey does not have as much experience as an average nurse but she has a comparatively higher education than an average nurse (see her education). So, her salary might be around that level, maybe a tad more.

Apart from being a nurse, Hailey is also an amateur musician. She has also released a song called “Make You Walk Away”. And you listen to her song on Spotify as well as on Hearnow.
