Who Is Yasin Cengiz And What Happened To The Tummy Dancer?

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Yasin Cengiz is a social media star from Turkey, officially known as the Republic of Türkiye. He has gained millions of fans on TikTok due to his viral belly dance, which earned him the nickname “Tummy Dancer.” His famous dance has made the 2022 song Dom Dom Yes Yes by Bulgarian singer Biser King more popular.

In early 2023, rumors of Yasin’s death began circulating on social media, with some alleging that he had died from a drug overdose and others claiming that he had perished in the February 2023 Turkey earthquake, which claimed thousands of lives and displaced millions of people. Fans of the Tummy Dancer were devastated by the news and took to social media to mourn him with the hashtag “RIP Yasin Cengiz.”

However, Cengiz debunked the rumors, saying he was alive. Here’s what we’ve gathered about him and what happened to him.

Biography Of Yasin Cengiz

Specifics about his parents and siblings are also undisclosed. This suggests that he is a private individual who prefers to keep his personal life confidential and away from public scrutiny.

He Joined TikTok In 2021 And Rose To Fame With His Tummy Dance

Yasin Cengiz began sharing his videos on the platform in 2021. On March 5, 2021, he uploaded his first video, in which he could be seen dancing with his belly to the tune of Kubilay Çetin ÖZTÜRK’s song, with the caption “tüm emektrlara gelsin,” which translates to “all veterans” in English. The video immediately gained popularity, receiving 278 thousand likes and numerous comments.

Ten days later, on March 15, 2021, Yasin uploaded another video of him doing the same belly dance, but this time with the caption “bir pazartesi sabahı neşeli işe başlama yolları,” which means “Cheerful ways to start work on a Monday morning” in English. This video also got over 200 thousand likes and many comments.

After receiving an excellent reaction to his initial videos, he continued to upload more videos, usually in his work clothes while working on the potato farm. His videos gained more traction, and his popularity began to soar.

In April 2022, Yasin uploaded another video where he could be seen dancing to Bulgarian singer Biser King’s song Dom Dom Yes Yes, while doing his belly dance. The video immediately went viral, receiving nearly a million likes and over 30 million views. This video catapulted Yasin’s popularity even further, and he continued to use Biser’s song in most of his TikTok videos, making the song even more popular.

Right now, it is not out of place to say Cengiz’s TikTok journey has been remarkable, and he has gained a considerable following on the platform. His dance moves, particularly his belly dance, have become a signature style in his videos. His content is unique, and his videos have resonated with people, making him one of the most sought-after creators on the platform in Turkey

At the moment, Yasin’s TikTok account has more than 14 million followers and is still counting. And because the belly dance became his signature, his TikTok fans nicknamed him the “Tummy Dancer.”

Yasin Cengiz Didn’t Die As Alleged In 2023

Cengiz was at the peak of his fame when two rumors about his death surfaced in early 2023. However, the Tummy Dancer himself later debunked the rumors and assured his fans that he was alive and well.

In January 2023, Yasin was first rumored to have died from a drug overdose, and traces of heroin were found in his system. This news came as a shock to his fans, who couldn’t believe that their idol had passed away in such a manner. However, the Turkish TikToker later posted on Instagram that he was alive and well, much to the relief of his fans.

Interestingly, there was a second wave of rumors about his death when the first had not yet dissipated. This time around, he was reported to have died in the deadly February 2023 Turkey earthquake, which affected an estimated 9.1 million people, with 3 million displaced and 50,783 deaths. However, like in the first case, Yasin came out again to debunk the fake news and assure his fans that he was alive and healthy.
