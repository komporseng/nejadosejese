Sprung Season 1 Review:

According to Rotten Tomatoes, the TV show “Sprung” is not afraid to be a little offbeat and eccentric, demonstrating the creator’s genuine fondness for these characters. The series ambitiously tackles many subjects, including the tumultuous experience we all went through in the first few years of the COVID-19 pandemic.

This portrayal instantly places the show in a specific timeframe, forming a significant hurdle that “Sprung” struggles to overcome. Nevertheless, this lighthearted comedy delightfully revolves around a group of individuals who are released from prison early due to the pandemic, with the global health crisis as a prominent backdrop rather than the central focus.

In summary, “Sprung” playfully explores the quirks of its characters while genuinely capturing the essence of the COVID-19 era, making it an amusing yet insightful comedy.
