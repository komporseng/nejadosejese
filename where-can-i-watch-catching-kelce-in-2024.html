Where Can I watch Catching Kelce in 2024?

June 2024 · 4 minute read

If you're a fan of the dating competition series "Catching Kelce," featuring Kansas City Chiefs' superstar tight end Travis Kelce, you may be wondering where you can watch the show in 2024.

Here's a detailed guide on the available platforms, streaming services, or networks where you can access the show, along with specific details such as subscription requirements, pricing, and additional features or benefits offered by each platform.

Peacock TV

The rights for "Catching Kelce" have been acquired by Peacock TV, making it available for streaming on this platform.

Peacock TV is a popular streaming service and is currently only available in the US. The subscription to Peacock TV starts at just $5.99 per month, and it offers a wide range of TV shows and movies in addition to "Catching Kelce".

Subscription Requirements and Pricing

  • To access "Catching Kelce" on Peacock TV, a subscription to the platform is required.
  • The subscription starts at $5.99 per month, offering access to a variety of content along with "Catching Kelce."

Additional Information

  • Peacock TV provides a diverse range of entertainment options, including original series, movies, news, and sports content.
  • Subscribers to Peacock TV can enjoy a user-friendly interface and the flexibility to stream their favorite shows on various devices.

Hayu

For viewers living in Canada or France, "Catching Kelce" can be streamed on the streaming service Hayu. Hayu is region-locked to Canada and France, and it offers a subscription starting at CAD$6.99 in Canada and €4.99 per month in France.

Subscription Requirements and Pricing

  • To watch "Catching Kelce" on Hayu, a subscription to the streaming service is necessary.
  • The subscription pricing starts at CAD$6.99 per month in Canada and €4.99 per month in France.

Additional Information

  • Hayu offers a vast collection of reality TV shows and documentaries, making it an ideal platform for fans of unscripted content.
  • Subscribers to Hayu can enjoy a seamless streaming experience and access to a diverse range of reality programming.

Apple TV

Apple TV is another platform where "Catching Kelce" may be available for streaming. The availability of the show on Apple TV has been spotted in Australia, and it may also be accessible in a few other regions. 

Subscription Requirements and Pricing
