Talented and fan-favorite Joey Batey is back as Jaskier in The Witcher season 2. Fans want to know if the actor/singer is dating a girlfriend and we have got some details for you.

Well renowned as Jaskier from the hit Netflix show The Witcher, Joey Batey is a British actor and also a musician; vocalist, and frontman of an indie band named The Amazing Devil. Batey has been successful in making a name for himself through his role in The Witcher, singing the tune for Geralt of Rivia. An incredibly talented actor who hustled all the way and found the best role that suits his persona, Joey won the hearts of many. Now many are interested to know more about the actor, especially about his girlfriend.

Born in Newcastle upon Tyne in 1989, Joey Batey grew up in a "musical family" and has always been around music. He co-wrote and performed the sketch show Good Clean Men with Alex Owen and Ben Ashenden (The Pin). After a few weeks at L'École Internationale de Théâtre Jacques Lecoq, he dropped out. Thus never actually been officially a trained actor.

In 2013, Batey made his film debut in Geoffrey Sax's British thriller Murder on the Home Front. Following 2014, he starred in Lone Scherfig's film The Riot Club. Later in 2017, he was cast as Eddie in Paul McGuigan's film Film Stars Don't Die in Liverpool. Batey has also been in the British television shows C.B. Strike (2017), In The Dark (2017), and Knightfall (2017). Starring in The Witcher was a triumph for the actor. The Witcher Season 2 Sees Jaskier Bringing Two New Songs

Few would have predicted that one of the most memorable moments from season one of The Witcher, which premiered on Netflix in 2019, would be the absolute banger of a song strummed by humble bard Jaskier played by Joey Batey in the second episode, rather than the special effects, fight choreography, or even Henry Cavill's central performance. Toss a Coin to Your Witcher's viral success surprised Batey and the song's creators; composers Giona Ostinelli and Sonya Belousova, as well as lyricist Jenny Klein.

Coming with another banger in season 2, released on Netflix on December 19, Batey followed up the season with the grittier Burn Butcher, Burn, performed in Episode 4. Jaskier's song is darker in tone than his previous, focusing on how he felt after his last encounter with Geralt of Rivia, in which the Witcher and bard parted ways on poor terms.

When asked how did he compose Burn Butcher, Burn, Batey explains, "The main thing that we wanted to do coming into Season 2 was explore Jaskier's discography and his musicality, and how he can shift and change and develop his own musical style," in an interview with Newsweek. He further added he wanted Jaskier to "step away from perhaps the poppier, catchy tunes and no longer rely on the stories of others to translate into the songs but rather explore his own stories and his own."

Will the song be a hit like his first Toss a Coin to Your Witcher, or will it be just another tune playing in the world-famous series? The Witcher season 2 is currently streaming on Netflix. The cast of the series is led by Henry Cavill as Geralt of Rivia, Anya Chalotra as Yennefer, and Freya Allan as Ciri.
