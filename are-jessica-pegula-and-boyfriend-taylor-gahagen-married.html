Are Jessica Pegula and Boyfriend Taylor Gahagen Married? A Deep Dive Into Their Long Term Relationship

Jessica Pegula and her boyfriend-turned-husband, Taylor Gahagen, are said to be married, as per sources. Additionally, they married at the magnificent Biltmore Estate in North Carolina in October of 2021.

Only their closest friends and family graced the occasion, which was kept extremely secret. Taylor Townsend, Samantha Crawford, Jennifer Brady, and Asia Muhammad, as well as other members of the Women’s Tennis Association, were in attendance.

Their three dogs, of course, were also present for the celebrations, posing for official wedding photos.

Further, the pair also arranged a wedding photoshoot in Paris, featuring many images of them in front of the Eiffel Tower.

Who Is Jessica Pegula’s Boyfriend Taylor Gahagen?

Taylor Gahagen is an American chief investment specialist who formerly worked for Jessica’s parents’ firm, Pegula Sports and Entertainment, for seven years.

After that, he worked with the Pegulas’ NHL professional hockey team, the Buffalo Sabres, as a director of corporate development, head of the brand strategy, and subsequently in marketing.

Besides, Gahagen earned an MBA from Canisius College and a bachelor’s degree in business administration from the State University of New York at Fredonia.

The 28-year-old is also recognized for his animal charity activities, having co-founded A Lending Paw, an animal welfare organization that helps rescue dogs and rehabilitates them for new homes.
