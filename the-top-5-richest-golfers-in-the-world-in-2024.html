The top 5 Richest Golfers in the World in 2024

September 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Golf is undeniably one of the richest sports globally, with top golfers amassing significant wealth from their professional careers, endorsements, and investments.

With the emergence of lucrative deals such as those offered by LIV Golf, the financial landscape of golf has seen dynamic changes in recent years.

As of January 2024, the top 5 richest golfers in the world are Tiger Woods, Greg Norman, Jack Nicklaus, Phil Mickelson, and Rory McIlroy.

1. Tiger Woods: $1.1 Billion

Tiger Woods continues to hold the status of being the richest golfer in the world with a reported net worth of approximately $1.1 billion as of January 2024.

His total career earnings amount to an impressive $1.7 billion, making him the only active athlete whose net worth counts in billions of dollars after LeBron James.

Despite playing in limited tournaments last season, Woods' off-course earnings and endorsements have significantly contributed to his immense wealth.

2. Greg Norman: $400 Million

Greg Norman, the CEO of LIV Golf, is among the wealthiest golfers globally. With a reported net worth of $400 million, Norman has not only amassed significant wealth from his professional career but also from his off-course earnings and investments.

3. Jack Nicklaus: $400 Million

Jack Nicklaus, another prominent figure in the golfing world, boasts a reported net worth of $400 million. In addition to his $1.15 billion in career earnings, Nicklaus has added substantial wealth as a golf course designer and brand endorser. 

4. Phil Mickelson: $300 Million

Phil Mickelson, a six-time Major winner, has accumulated considerable wealth from his decades-long professional career, with an estimated net worth of around $300 million.

His impressive career earnings, as well as his off-course earnings and endorsements, have solidified his position as one of the richest golfers in the world in 2024.

5. Rory McIlroy: $170 Million

Rory McIlroy rounds up the top 5 richest golfers in the world with a reported net worth of approximately $170 million.

As the 15th highest-paid athlete of 2023, McIlroy's career earnings totaling $140,135,058 have been further augmented by his off-course earnings, including substantial amounts from official and unofficial tournaments, as well as the Player Index Program.

 

The world of golf continues to witness the immense financial success of its top players, with significant contributions from their professional careers, off-course earnings, endorsements, and investments.
