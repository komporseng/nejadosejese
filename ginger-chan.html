Career and Professional Life

Caption: Ginger posing for a photo with her family at her workstation. Source: Instagram

Professionally, Ginger Chan is a traffic anchor for KTLA. She is also famous as the on-air reporter alongside Ryan Seacrest on 102.7 KIIS-FM.

This personality joined the KTLA network in March 2008. Initially, she worked as a traffic and news reporter. At the beginning of this career, she flew in the helicopter, Sky5. At present, Chan helps commuters steer clear of collisions. She also assists them in construction projects every weekday in the morning from 4 a.m. to 10 a.m.

Before this workstation, she worked at KIIS-FM. There, she reported both the traffic as well as covered the news as a fill-in for Ellen K. Back then, this personality spent her mornings as well as afternoons flying over the skies of Southern California. She flew in a Cessna 172 and covered traffic for shows on KLAC-AM. Moreover, she also worked with popular personalities like Matt “Money” Smith, T.J. Simers, Fred Roggin, and Petros Papadakis.

Additionally, this reporter also anchored the weekend news for KFI-AM along with completing her traffic duties. KFI-AM is a top-rated talk radio station. Further, this personality’s experience also includes working as a radio personality or jockeying. She previously worked for California stations in Redlands, Bakersfield, and San Diego.

At present, we can catch this personality from the TV to the radio every weekday morning. Furthermore, she is also a part of the “On-Air with Ryan Seacrest” that airs on KIIS-FM.
