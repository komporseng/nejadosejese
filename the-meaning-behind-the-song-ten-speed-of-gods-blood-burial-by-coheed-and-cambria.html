The Story of Ten Speed (Of God’s Blood & Burial)

“Ten Speed (Of God’s Blood & Burial)” is a song that takes listeners on a complex and mysterious journey. The track is part of an overarching concept known as “The Amory Wars,” which serves as the basis for many of Coheed and Cambria’s songs. This concept revolves around a fictional universe and its characters, and the album “In Keeping Secrets of Silent Earth: 3” is a crucial part of this narrative.

The song’s lyrics depict a protagonist facing a critical dilemma – choosing between a ten-speed bicycle that symbolizes freedom and a religious sect known as the “Order of Wilhelmina.” This choice becomes a metaphorical struggle between personal liberation and religious devotion. The lyrics are imbued with poetic imagery, showcasing the band’s talent for storytelling.
