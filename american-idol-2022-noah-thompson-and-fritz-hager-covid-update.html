Noah Thompson And Fritz Hager Covid Update: American Idol 2022 Contestant - Where Are They Now

June 2024 · 4 minute read

American Idol 2022: Noah Thompson And Fritz Hager Covid Update – The May 8 episode of “American Idol” began with Ryan Seacrest informing viewers that Fritz Hager and Noah Thompson had tested positive for COVID-19.

Noah Thompson is a singer and songwriter from the United States who rose to fame after appearing on the 20th season of ABC’s singing competition show American Idol.

Fritz Hager is a 22-year-old security guard from Tyler, Texas, who competed on American Idol 2022 as a vocalist.

American Idol 2022: Noah Thompson And Fritz Hager Covid Update

Fritz Hager and Noah Thompson tested positive for the coronavirus prior to the May 8 episode of American Idol. The news was delivered by host Ryan Seacrest at the start of the broadcast.

Ryan stated that Thompson had become unwell earlier in the week and would be performing virtually from his hotel room. Hager’s symptoms emerged closer to the live show, therefore his rehearsal video had to be utilized for his two appearances.

Hager opted to perform an original song for his TikTok video. Even though it was merely a rehearsal video, Fritz’s performance delighted the judges.

Because of Hager’s COVID diagnosis, the show broadcast practice footage from the day before instead of his playing live for the audience and judges.

Where Are Noah Thompson And Fritz Hager Now -Health Update

Fritz Hager and Noah Thompson are resting in a hotel room after being diagnosed with the coronavirus and receiving medical treatment.

Fritz expressed his happiness to viewers from his hotel room. It seems like a jail in the hotel room, with the goal of getting back on stage. He stated that he is simply napping a lot to pass the time.

Noah then stated that he is getting by man and that it stinks. He is, nevertheless, making progress.

HunterGirl, Christian Guardino, Jay Copeland, Leah Marlene, and Nicolina are the remaining five contestants still on the show. All five were COVID-19-free and ready to go live.

Ryan inquired as to what Hager was doing to pass the time. ‘I’m simply lying in bed asleep,’ Hager explained.

American Idol Top 5: Noah Thompson And Fritz Hager

Fritz Hager and Noah Thompson were two of the top seven competitors that performed two songs in the competition, as well as two of the five voted into the Top 5.

Fritz began singing at a young age and is a member of Tyler Civic Theater Center, and other shows such as West Side Story and Newsies. During the American Idol tryouts, he sang Before You Go by Lewis Capaldi and received the Golden Ticket.

Noah grew up watching his father play in a small-town Southern rock band, and Noah performed on Kameron Marlowe’s song Giving You Up at the audition, stunning the judges.
