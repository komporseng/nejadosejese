The Kelce brothers, Jason and Travis, are notable figures in the world of professional sports. Jason Kelce is an American football center for the Philadelphia Eagles, while Travis Kelce is a tight end for the Kansas City Chiefs. Aside from their successful football careers, the Kelce brothers are known for their vibrant and distinctive hairstyles. In this article, we will explore what happened to Jason and Travis Kelce's hair and how their hairstyles have become iconic.

Jason Kelce's Hair Evolution

Early Career

Jason Kelce began his NFL career in 2011 when he was drafted by the Philadelphia Eagles. At that time, he sported a clean-cut and conventional hairstyle, similar to many athletes in the league. However, as his career progressed, Kelce started experimenting with his hair and began growing out his beard.

The Super Bowl Season

One of the most memorable moments in Jason Kelce's career was his performance during the Philadelphia Eagles' Super Bowl LII victory in 2018. Kelce's passionate and emotional speech during the victory parade became a defining moment not only for him but also for Eagles fans. He wore a colorful and elaborate Mummer-inspired costume during the parade, complete with a matching wig and face paint.

The Mullet Era

Following the Super Bowl win, Jason Kelce decided to keep his hair long and embraced the mullet hairstyle. He trimmed the sides of his head while leaving the back long and flowing. The mullet became his signature look, and it quickly gained recognition among fans and the media.

Charity Efforts

In addition to his football career, Jason Kelce has also used his iconic hairstyle to raise awareness for charitable causes. He has often dyed and styled his hair in support of various charitable initiatives. For instance, he dyed his hair green to raise awareness for mental health and autism during Autism Awareness Month.

Travis Kelce's Hair Journey

Rookie Seasons

Travis Kelce entered the NFL in 2013 when he was drafted by the Kansas City Chiefs. Initially, he sported a more traditional look with short hair. However, as his career progressed, he began experimenting with different hairstyles.

The Dreadlocks

Travis Kelce gained significant attention when he decided to grow out his hair and sported dreadlocks. This hairstyle became his trademark, and it added to his unique persona on and off the field. The dreadlocks became synonymous with his energetic playing style and enthusiastic personality.

The Buzz Cut

After several seasons with dreadlocks, Travis Kelce surprised fans by shaving off his iconic hairstyle. He opted for a buzz cut, which was a drastic change from his previous look. This change sparked discussions among fans and media, with many speculating about the reasons behind the haircut.

Return to the Dreadlocks

To the delight of many fans, Travis Kelce eventually returned to his beloved dreadlocks. He grew out his hair once again, and the dreadlocks became a defining part of his image.

Jason and Travis Kelce's hairstyles have become an integral part of their public personas. From Jason's mullet to Travis' dreadlocks, their unique hairstyles have captured the attention of fans and media alike. These hairstyles have not only become iconic but have also allowed the Kelce brothers to express their individuality and support charitable causes. As their football careers continue to thrive, it will be intriguing to see how their hairstyles evolve and become even more intertwined with their identities.
